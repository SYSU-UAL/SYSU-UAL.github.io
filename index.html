Welcome to Urban Air Laboratory(UAL)!

Our research group focuses on urban meteorology and air pollution, specifically examining urban atmospheric flow and turbulence, urban ventilation and thermal environments, and urban organic gas emission and transport. We aim to provide scientific support for sustainable urban development, climate change adaptation, air quality improvement, and low-altitude flight safety. We hope to advance the optimization of urban environments and contribute to the formulation of effective policies to address the growing challenges of urban environmental issues.

本研究组研究方向是城市气象和空气污染，重点关注城市大气流场和湍流、城市通风和热环境、城市有机气体排放与传输等问题。我们的研究旨在为可持续城市建设、气候变化应对、空气质量提升及低空经济气象保障等国家需求提供科学支撑。我们期望能够推动城市环境的优化和相关政策的制定，以应对日益严峻的城市环境问题。
